Born:

        Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu (born 9 August 1975) 
        is an Indian actor, film producer, media 
        personality, and philanthropist who works in Telugu cinema.

Occupation:

        Prabhas, is an Indian actor who works predominantly 
        in Telugu cinema. Referred to in the media as the 
        "Rebel Star", he is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema.
